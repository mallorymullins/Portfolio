Proposal:

"If You're Bored Button" will be an application to randomly present a javascript based game everytime you click on the button. This is a similar idea to a popular already existing application called boredbutton. The difference will be that the games will all be hosted under our application and does not link external sites. Some of the games that we use will be made by us. We may plan on implementing other games as well, also a gallery page to select specific games at will. The front end of our development will include an aesthetic and functional UI, the backend and database development will entail hosting, storing player data, and providing the javascript games.

We decided to make a randomized game selector, as in quarantine times are tough an it is easy to get bored. This is a quick and fun idea for people to play small games as a bit of a time waster. Having it be randomized allows you to keep it spicey and new without having to do all the searching.
